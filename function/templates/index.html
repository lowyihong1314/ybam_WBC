About the Conference

In an era where artificial intelligence reshapes every aspect of human experience, the World Buddhist Conference 2025 brings together the timeless wisdom of the Dharma with contemporary technological challenges. This gathering represents a unique opportunity to explore how Buddhist principles can guide ethical AI development and mindful technology use.

Our conference bridges ancient contemplative traditions with modern innovation, fostering dialogue between monastic communities, lay practitioners, researchers, and technology leaders. Together, we will examine how compassion, mindfulness, and wisdom can inform our relationship with AI and digital transformation.

Join us as we cultivate understanding, share insights, and develop frameworks for integrating Buddhist values into our increasingly connected world, ensuring technology serves the flourishing of all sentient beings.

Conference Themes

Main Theme: AI, Buddhism and Buddhist Life

Exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence with Buddhist philosophy, ethics, and daily practice in the modern world.

AI Ethics & Compassion in Practice

Examining how Buddhist principles of compassion and non-harm can guide ethical AI development. We'll explore frameworks for ensuring AI systems embody wisdom and serve the welfare of all beings.

Mindfulness, Mental Health, and Digital Well-being

Investigating the role of mindfulness practices in maintaining mental health within digital environments. Discussions will cover meditation apps, digital detox, and cultivating presence in an always-connected world.

Monastic & Lay Perspectives on Technology

Bridging traditional monastic wisdom with contemporary lay practice in technological contexts. Exploring how different Buddhist communities adapt ancient teachings for modern digital life.

Workshops

Mindful AI Development

Objective: Learn to integrate Buddhist principles into AI system design and development processes.

Learning Outcomes: Participants will understand ethical frameworks, practice mindful coding techniques, and develop compassionate AI guidelines.

Audience: Developers, researchers, and technology professionals interested in ethical AI.

Digital Detox & Contemplative Practice

Objective: Explore methods for maintaining contemplative practice while engaging with digital technology.

Learning Outcomes: Develop personal digital boundaries, learn tech-free meditation techniques, and create sustainable practice routines.

Audience: Practitioners seeking balance between technology use and spiritual development.

Teaching Dharma in Digital Spaces

Objective: Master effective methods for sharing Buddhist teachings through online platforms and digital media.

Learning Outcomes: Create engaging online content, understand virtual community building, and adapt traditional teachings for digital formats.

Audience: Teachers, monastics, and dharma practitioners interested in online outreach.

AI-Assisted Meditation & Mindfulness

Objective: Evaluate and utilize AI tools that support meditation practice while maintaining authentic dharma transmission.

Learning Outcomes: Assess meditation apps critically, understand AI limitations in spiritual guidance, and integrate helpful technologies mindfully.

Audience: Meditation practitioners and teachers exploring technology-assisted practice.
