About YBAM

The Young Buddhist Association of Malaysia (YBAM) is the leading Buddhist youth organization in Malaysia. Since its establishment in 1970, YBAM has upheld the mission of compassion, growth and liberation based on the teachings of the Buddha, promoting positive lifestyles and building a society rooted in wisdom, compassion and gratitude.


YBAM actively supports the development of Buddhist organizations nationwide, promoting Dhamma education among youth and providing leadership training opportunities. With YBAM‚Äôs foundational support, local Buddhist organizations have made remarkable progress in nurturing Buddhist communities across Malaysia.


YBAM coordinates over 270 member organizations across Malaysia through its 13 State Liaison Committees. It also supports university Buddhist societies, high school Buddhist societies, and youth Buddhist fellowships. YBAM is the only nationally recognized Buddhist youth organization acknowledged by the Malaysian government.
